Coaching/Mechanics Of Dragon Ball FighterZ



Dragon ball Z fighter is a fighting and adventure game that is based on the dragon ball series. In the game you can play many different and strong characters whether that be against a player or a bot. For each character in the game they will have unique moves that belong to them that can allow for different game-play for every different character you play. Also, you can play a story mod that somewhat goes over the original dragon ball series stories. The appropriate rated age for this game is 11 with an audience ranking of 4.2. If you are starting as a Noob in the game you may find my website helpful because it would allow you to learn the basic mechanics of the game and how to play with different characters. The way I play the game is that I memorize the buttons of the controller and what they do, so that when I play a different character I would know how to play with them even if it is my first time playing with the character. The way you get better at the game is by memorizing the type of move sets in the game and how to use them effectively, then practice those moves for some time to get them right. A strategy that I would suggest to people who are starting the game would be when you start to combo and you feel it will end, switch to another character to allow the combo to continue. As a whole the difficulty of the game would probably be put at difficult or higher if you were to pick it up because of how you have to memorize and put into play new combos for your character which is not easy and takes time.
